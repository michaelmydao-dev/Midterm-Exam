Michael Dao

As a multidisciplinary designer with a primary focus on digital graphic design, my work is playful and exploratory. Rather than a concrete or fixed aesthetic, my work aims to celebrate the diversity of visual styles. Different styles from various themes or movements stand as lasting contributions that carry history and context to their work. Through studying, understanding, and referencing these visual cultures, my work in Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop begins to incorporate the reasoning behind principles and revisits nuance with intention. This approach of respect allows me to develop communication between one’s taste and execution.
